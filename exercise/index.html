welcome to ABC BASE CAMP

ABC base camp refers to Annapurna Base Camp, a renowned trekking destination in Nepal located at 4,130 meters (13,549 feet) in the Annapurna Conservation Area. The trek offers breathtaking 360-degree views of Himalayan peaks, including Annapurna I and Machhapuchhre (Fishtail), diverse landscapes, lush forests, and a glimpse into the local Gurung culture. While possible year-round, the best times to trek are the spring (March-May) and autumn (September-November) seasons.
